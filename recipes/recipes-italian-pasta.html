Lemon Basil Creamy Pasta

30 minutes Serves 4 350 cal
#Pasta #Vegan #Lunch #Healthy

This homemade Lemon Basil Creamy Pasta is a refreshing twist on comfort food! With its zesty lemon flavor, fresh basil, and a rich, creamy sauce, it's easy to make and perfect for a quick, satisfying meal. Bright, vibrant, and full of flavor, it's sure to become a new favorite!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 cups cashews, soaked overnight
  • 1 cup plant-based milk (unsweetened)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tablespoons nutritional yeast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Pasta

Cook the fettuccine according to package instructions in salted water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining.

Step 2: Make the Sauce

In a high-speed blender, combine soaked cashews, plant milk, garlic, lemon juice and zest, half of the basil leaves, and nutritional yeast. Blend until completely smooth.

Step 3: Cook the Sauce

Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Pour in the cashew sauce and simmer for 5 minutes until it starts to thicken.

Step 4: Combine

Add the cooked pasta to the sauce, tossing to combine. Add pasta water as needed to reach desired consistency. Stir in remaining fresh basil leaves.

Nutritional Information

Per Serving:

  • Calories: 350
  • Protein: 12g
  • Carbohydrates: 45g
  • Fat: 14g
  • Fiber: 4g
  • Sodium: 380mg

Tips

  • Soak cashews overnight for the creamiest sauce
  • Use fresh basil for the best flavor
  • Reserve pasta water - it's essential for achieving the perfect sauce consistency
  • Toast some pine nuts for an optional garnish

Storage

Store in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. When reheating, add a splash of plant milk or water to restore the creamy consistency.
