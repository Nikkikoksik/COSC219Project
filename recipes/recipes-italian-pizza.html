Homemade Italian Pizza

30 minutes Serves 4 450 cal
#Italian #Pizza #Dinner #Vegetarian

This classic homemade vegetarian pizza combines the perfect crispy crust with rich tomato sauce and fresh Mediterranean vegetables. Topped with fresh basil and premium Italian cheese, this pizza brings authentic Italian flavors right to your kitchen. Perfect for family dinners or entertaining guests!

Ingredients

For the Pizza Dough:

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water

For the Toppings:

  • 1 cup homemade tomato sauce
  • 2 cups fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 bell pepper, thinly sliced
  • 1 red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 cups fresh baby spinach
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions

Step 1: Prepare the Dough

Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a large bowl. Add olive oil and warm water. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic. Place in an oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Step 2: Prepare Toppings

While dough rises, slice vegetables and prepare tomato sauce. Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.

Step 3: Shape and Top

Punch down dough and roll into a 14-inch circle. Spread tomato sauce, leaving a 1/2-inch border. Layer with mozzarella, vegetables, and seasonings.

Step 4: Bake

Transfer pizza to preheated stone or sheet. Bake for 15-20 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly. Top with fresh basil and Parmesan.

Nutritional Information

Per Serving (1/4 pizza):

  • Calories: 450
  • Protein: 18g
  • Carbohydrates: 52g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Fiber: 3g
  • Sodium: 890mg

Tips

  • Let dough come to room temperature before shaping
  • Preheat oven for at least 30 minutes with the pizza stone
  • Don't overload with toppings to ensure crispy crust
  • Brush the crust with olive oil before baking for extra crispiness

Storage

Store leftover pizza in an airtight container in the refrigerator for up to 3 days. Reheat in a preheated oven at 375°F for 10 minutes to restore crispiness. For best results, don't microwave.
